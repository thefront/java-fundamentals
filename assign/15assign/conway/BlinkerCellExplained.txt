The new BlinkerCell is a cell that looks like a cross. You will be prompted to
enter the location of the center cell. Once placed, the 3 cells will blink on
and off with the center one always on ("alive"). The original 5 ConwayCells 
will morph based on the location of your new BlinkerCell.
